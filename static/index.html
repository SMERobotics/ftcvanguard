Core Capabilities

Real-Time Dashboard

Live schedule, scores, and rankings that remain current while you lock in. Stay ahead of the crowd with instant updates from the field.

Collaborative Scouting

One unified shared notes system for your whole team. Pit intel shows up instantly for the whole gang.

Direct Insights

Clearly analyze breakdowns and trends that help you pick partners, set roles, and walk into matches with a plan.
