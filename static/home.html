Core Capabilities

Live Match Tracking

Real-time queuing status, score breakdowns, and countdown timers. Stay ahead of the schedule with instant updates from the field.

Rankings & OPR

Live leaderboard with automated OPR, Ranking Score, and detailed W-L-T records. Know exactly where every team stands.

Collaborative Scouting

Unified database for your entire team. Notes entered in the pits are visible to the rest of the team, instantly.

Predictive Analytics

Interactive charts showing performance trends, auto vs. teleop breakdowns, and historical data for every team in the event. Get an idea of how things have changed over time.

Strategy Canvas

Integrated field drawing tool with draggable robot tokens and phase-specific boards. Export your game plan as a PNG.

Always Free

Vanguard is open-source and free to use. Host it yourself or use our cloud version with zero hidden costs or subscriptions.
