Capabilities

Everything You Need at the Competition

Six integrated modules designed for the speed and pressure of live FTC events.

Live Schedule

See your upcoming matches with countdown timers, live scores, and detailed score breakdowns with composition charts.

Rankings

Sortable leaderboard with Ranking Score, OPR, Match Points, Auto Points, High Score, and Win-Loss-Tie records.

Scouting Notes

Collaborative per-team scouting with auto, teleop, and general fields. Auto-saves with stale-note indicators across your whole team.

Team Insights

Performance analytics with interactive charts showing score trends, auto vs teleop breakdowns, and cross-event history for any team.

Strategy Planner

Canvas-based field drawing tool with per-phase strategy boards, draggable robot tokens, color picker, and exportable PNG output.

Push Notifications

Automatic alerts for match queueing, 5-minute warnings, and schedule availability sent directly to your device via ntfy.sh.

Interface

Built for the Pits

A dark, information-dense interface designed for rapid data access under competition pressure.
