Vanguard accounts are manually approved to verify each team is who they say they are. Once approved, you will receive a temporary password via email.

Please include a clear image of your robot with team number signs visible and a piece of paper that says "Vanguard".

  • Approval usually happens quickly during the day (Chicago time)
  • It will make you reset your temporary password after first login
  • Use an image host like Imgur or Google Drive with public access
  • It doesn't have to be together! If you're in the middle of a rebuild and your bot is in pieces, just lean a number sign on that and you're good! Alternatively, DM us on instagram from your team account at @vanguardftc, or talk to one of us at a competition. (Teams 26855 & 6547)
